About Me

A tech enthusiast with a solid background in IT support and a strong drive to transition into a development role. My journey in tech began in IT support, where I worked with multiple clients, troubleshooting network issues, managing software and hardware configurations, and ensuring smooth, secure operations for end-users. This experience taught me the importance of problem-solving, communication, and adaptability—qualities that I’m excited to carry forward into a development role.

Working closely with software and systems every day sparked my interest in creating and building technology solutions, not just supporting them. Driven by this curiosity, I’ve been dedicating my time to learning and diving into full-stack development. I’m already experimenting with personal projects, contributing to open-source, and refining my skills through hands-on coding challenges.

What excites me most about becoming a developer is the opportunity to create impactful solutions from the ground up, combining my technical understanding from IT support with programming skills. I’m eager to join a team where I can bring my dedication to problem-solving, my eye for detail, and my commitment to continuous learning.

When I’m not coding, I enjoy spending time with my cat, or experimenting with new tools or stuffs that exicts me, which keeps me inspired and motivated. I’m thrilled to be on this journey and can’t wait to make a meaningful contribution as a developer.
